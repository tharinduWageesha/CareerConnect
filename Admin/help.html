Managing Employees

Adding a New Employee

To add a new employee to the system:

  • Click on "Manage Employees" in the navigation menu
  • Click the green "Add New Employee" button at the bottom
  • Fill in all required fields: Name, Email, and Password
  • Click "Save Employee" to create the account

Important Notes

Email addresses must be unique. If you try to add an employee with an existing email, the system will show an error.

Editing Employee Information

To modify an existing employee's details:

  • Go to "Manage Employees"
  • Find the employee in the table
  • Click the green "Edit" button in the Actions column
  • Modify the information as needed
  • Leave the password field empty to keep the current password
  • Click "Update Employee" to save changes

Deleting Employees

To remove an employee from the system:

  • Navigate to "Manage Employees"
  • Find the employee you want to remove
  • Click the red "Delete" button
  • Confirm the deletion in the popup dialog

Warning

Deleting an employee is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure you really want to remove this account before confirming.

Managing Companies

Adding a New Company

To register a new company:

  • Click "Manage Companies" in the navigation
  • Click "Add a company" button
  • Fill in all company details:
    • Company Name
    • Email Address
    • Industry
    • Location
    • Password for company login
  • Click "Save Company" to add to the system

Editing Company Information

To update company details:

  • Go to "Manage Companies"
  • Click "Edit" next to the company you want to modify
  • Update the required fields
  • Leave password blank to keep existing password
  • Click "Update Company" to save

Removing Companies

To delete a company profile:

  • Navigate to "Manage Companies"
  • Click "Delete" next to the company
  • Confirm the deletion when prompted

Account Management

Viewing Your Profile

Click "My Account" to view your admin profile information including name, email, role, and last login time.

Editing Your Profile

To update your admin account:

  • Go to "My Account"
  • Click "Edit Profile" button
  • Modify your name or email address
  • Optionally enter a new password
  • Click "Update Profile" to save changes

Changing Your Password

Security & Best Practices

Password Security

Follow these guidelines for secure passwords:

  • Use at least 8 characters
  • Include uppercase and lowercase letters
  • Add numbers and special characters
  • Avoid common words or personal information
  • Change passwords regularly

Safe Logout

Always use the "Log Out" button when finished to ensure your session is properly closed and protect your account from unauthorized access.

Security Features

The system automatically protects against common security threats including SQL injection and cross-site scripting (XSS) attacks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What happens if I enter a duplicate email address?
A: The system will display an error message as email addresses must be unique for both employees and companies.
Q: Can I recover deleted employees or companies?
A: No, deletions are permanent. Always double-check before confirming a deletion.
Q: Why don't I see a "Cancel" button on edit forms?
A: Use your browser's back button or click on the navigation menu to return to the previous page without saving changes.
Q: Can employees and companies have the same email address?
A: No, email addresses must be unique across the entire system to prevent login conflicts.
Q: How do I know if changes were saved successfully?
A: The system will redirect you back to the management page and display a success message when changes are saved.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues and Solutions

Problem: Page doesn't load or shows database error
Solution: Check your internet connection and try refreshing the page. If the problem persists, contact your system administrator.
Problem: Can't add new employee/company
Solution: Ensure all required fields are filled and the email address isn't already in use.
Problem: Delete confirmation popup doesn't appear
Solution: Make sure JavaScript is enabled in your browser.
Problem: Form submission fails
Solution: Check that all required fields are completed and try again.
